# Markdown version (paste into Buttondown's compose window)

Hi —

You signed up for The Symbiotic Mind. Thank you. That's not nothing in 2026.

We're Vlad Sterngold and Milena Nikolova. He builds AI systems and lives inside them daily, from Amsterdam. She is a behavioral scientist and runs BehaviorSMART, from Lausanne. Different angles on the same question: what is happening to the human, on the other side of the screen, when AI enters the work.

Most of the AI conversation is about the tool — its speed, its capabilities, its risks. We think the more interesting question is the one almost no one is asking out loud:

**AI × HI is not a tool usage question. It is a relationship design problem.**

The unit of value is the combined AI × HI system. It belongs to neither alone. And right now, most professionals are improvising that relationship instead of designing it.

That's what we'll write about, every other Wednesday.

### Two pieces to start

If you've read these already, skip them. If not, they're the foundation everything else builds on:

- [**You Are Not Using AI. You Are in a Relationship With It.**](https://symbiotic-mind.com/posts/001-you-are-not-using-ai/) — Milena, on why we say "please" to chatbots, and why it matters that we do
- [**My API, Not My Resume**](https://symbiotic-mind.com/posts/002-my-api-not-my-resume/) — Vlad, on what professional identity looks like when you arrive at a new job with a pre-built AI team

### What you'll get from us

- **New writing** — every other Wednesday. Long-form, full text in the email, not preview-plus-clickbait.
- **Conversations** — long-form audio episodes between us, starting later this season. The audio companion to the writing, on Apple, Spotify, Overcast, all the usual places.
- **Field notes** — short pieces on what's actually changing for practitioners. Things we noticed this week.

### What you won't get

- Daily emails. Engagement-bait subject lines. AI-generated filler. Affiliate links. "10 prompts that will change your life." We will never do those.

### One ask

Reply to this email. Tell us where you're reading from, what you do, and one question about AI × HI that's been on your mind. We read every reply, and the patterns shape what we write next.

Talk soon,

Vlad & Milena
