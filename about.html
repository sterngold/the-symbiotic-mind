About

AI × HI — The Symbiotic Mind is a writing project by Vlad Sterngold and Milena S. Nikolova, PhD.

We write about what happens when artificial and human intelligence meet — not as a technology question, but as a behavioral one. How do professionals change? How do we design the interaction so better thinking and decisions happen naturally?

Vlad Sterngold

AI practitioner and builder. Daily user of AI systems, builder of AI products, and writer on human-AI symbiosis. Based in Amsterdam.

Milena S. Nikolova, PhD

Behavioral scientist and founder of BehaviorSMART. Works with organizations on how humans actually change — and what AI does and doesn't change about that.

The position

Nobody else occupies the intersection of behavioral science, AI symbiosis, and business transformation. We bring both sides: Vlad as AI/tech practitioner, Milena as behavior credibility. Together: AI doesn't replace human behavior — it changes it. The question is whether you design that change or let it happen to you.
