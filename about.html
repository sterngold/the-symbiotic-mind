About

A writing project on human-AI symbiosis, behavioral science, and professional transformation.

AI × HI — The Symbiotic Mind is a writing project by Vlad Sterngold and Milena S. Nikolova, PhD. We write about what happens when artificial and human intelligence meet — not as a technology question, but as a behavioral one.

How do professionals change when AI enters the picture? How do we design that interaction so better thinking and decisions happen naturally? These are the questions that interest us. Not tools, not prompts — the relationship itself.

Vlad Sterngold

AI practitioner and builder based in Amsterdam. Daily user of AI systems, builder of AI products, and writer on human-AI symbiosis. Brings the practitioner's lens: what actually happens when you work closely with AI, and what it changes in how you think.

Milena S. Nikolova, PhD

Behavioral scientist and founder of BehaviorSMART. Works with organizations on how humans actually change — what AI does and doesn't change about that, and how to design for the difference. Brings the science: behavior is not a bug in the adoption curve, it is the curve.

The position

Nobody else occupies the intersection of behavioral science, AI symbiosis, and business transformation. AI doesn't replace human behavior — it changes it. The question is whether you design that change or let it happen to you.

Why we write

Because the conversation about AI is dominated by two failure modes: breathless optimism and breathless fear. We think there's a third frame — symbiosis — that is more honest, more useful, and more interesting. We are trying to build that frame in public, one piece at a time.
